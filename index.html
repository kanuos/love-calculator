Love prediction App

This app predicts the nature of emotion you have with your loved ones. It may be your friend or your companion.

This app works on the school yard FLAME and LAKME algorithms. FLAME and LAKME are acronyms.
FLAME stands for "FRIENDS", "LOVE","ATTRACTION","MARRIAGE","ENEMY","SIBLINGS"
LAKME stands for "LOVE","ATTRACTION","KISS","MARRIAGE","ENEMY"..

Enjoy!!!
